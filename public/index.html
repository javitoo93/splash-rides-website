Safety First

We take safety seriously

Every boat and captain on Splash is verified before they can accept a single booking.

Captain Verification

USCG license and government ID reviewed and approved by our team before any captain goes live.

Vessel Requirements

Current registration, required safety equipment, and adequate liability insurance — verified before listing.

Safety Briefings

Captains conduct a mandatory pre-departure briefing covering emergency procedures and safety equipment.

Weather Monitoring

Real-time weather alerts. Captains have full authority to cancel or postpone any trip for safety.

Secure Messaging

All communication is in-app. Personal contact sharing is blocked to protect everyone involved.

State Compliance

Owners must meet their state's insurance and permit requirements. We verify documentation before going live.
