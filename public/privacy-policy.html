1. Introduction

Splash Rides, Inc., a Delaware corporation (“Splash Rides,” “we,” “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile applications, website, and related services (the “Platform”). This policy applies to all Users in the United States.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Platform;
  • Process bookings and payments;
  • Verify Service Provider credentials and identities;
  • Facilitate communication between Renters and Service Providers;
  • Send booking confirmations, receipts, and service-related communications;
  • Detect, prevent, and investigate fraud, abuse, and security issues;
  • Comply with legal obligations and respond to lawful requests;
  • Enforce our Terms of Service;
  • Send promotional communications (only with your explicit consent; you may opt out at any time).

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only in these circumstances:

4.1 With Other Users

When you make a booking, limited information is shared between Renters and Service Providers (e.g., first name, profile photo, booking details) to facilitate the trip.

4.2 With Third-Party Service Providers

  • Stripe: Payment processing. Stripe’s privacy policy governs their handling of your payment data. Splash Rides never stores your full card number.
  • AWS: Cloud storage for uploaded documents and files.
  • Render: Application and database hosting.

We require all third-party service providers to maintain the confidentiality and security of your information.

4.3 For Legal Reasons

We may disclose information to: (a) comply with applicable law, regulation, or legal process; (b) enforce our Terms of Service; (c) protect the rights, property, or safety of Splash Rides, our Users, or the public; (d) respond to lawful requests by public authorities, including law enforcement.

4.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, reorganization, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you of any such change in advance.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (TLS/HTTPS);
  • Bcrypt password hashing with salt (cost factor 12);
  • JWT-based authentication with secure token management;
  • Rate limiting on authentication endpoints to prevent brute-force attacks;
  • Authenticated access to sensitive document uploads;
  • Timing-safe OTP verification;
  • Secure file storage on AWS S3 with access controls.

No system is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information using commercially reasonable measures, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

  • Account data: Duration of your account plus 3 years after deletion.
  • Booking records: 7 years (for tax, legal, and regulatory compliance).
  • Verification documents: Duration of active Service Provider status plus 1 year.
  • Messages: 2 years after the related booking, or upon account deletion.
  • Payment records: 7 years (as required by financial regulations).
  • Terms acceptance logs: Indefinitely (legal compliance and proof of consent).

7. Your Rights

Depending on your state of residence, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and personal data (subject to legal retention requirements).
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Opt-Out of Marketing: Unsubscribe from promotional communications at any time.
  • Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights.

To exercise these rights, email us at privacy@splash-inc.com. We will respond within 45 days.

8. State-Specific Rights

8.1 California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act and the California Privacy Rights Act, including: the right to know what personal information is collected and how it is used; the right to delete personal information; the right to correct inaccurate information; the right to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information; and the right to limit use of sensitive personal information. We do not sell or share personal information as defined by the CCPA/CPRA.

8.2 Virginia, Colorado, Connecticut, Utah, Texas, and Other State Privacy Laws

If you reside in a state with a comprehensive consumer privacy law, you may have rights similar to those described above. We honor all applicable state privacy rights. Contact us at privacy@splash-inc.com to exercise your rights.

9. Children’s Privacy

The Platform is strictly for users 18 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect information from individuals under 18. If we learn that information from a minor has been collected, we will delete it immediately. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us at privacy@splash-inc.com immediately.

10. Location Data

We collect precise location data only when you grant explicit permission through your device settings. You may revoke location access at any time through your device settings. Location data is used to show nearby boats, enable real-time trip tracking, and improve the Platform. We do not sell location data to any third party.

11. Do Not Track

Some browsers transmit “Do Not Track” signals. We currently do not respond to Do Not Track signals, as there is no industry-wide standard for compliance.

12. International Users

The Platform is intended for use within the United States. If you access the Platform from outside the United States, you do so at your own risk and are responsible for compliance with local laws. Your information will be transferred to and processed in the United States.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated via email or in-app notification at least 30 days before taking effect. If we update this policy, you will be required to review and accept the updated version before continuing to use the Platform.
