Provider Agreement

Version 2.1  |  Effective Date: March 28, 2026  |  Last Updated: March 28, 2026

This Provider Agreement supplements the Splash Rides Terms of Service and applies specifically to Boat Owners and Captains listing services on the Platform. By completing registration as a Service Provider, you agree to this Agreement in addition to the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

1. Independent Contractor Status

You are an independent contractor of Splash Rides, Inc. ("Splash Rides"), not an employee, agent, joint venturer, or partner. Nothing in this Agreement shall create an employment, agency, or partnership relationship. You are solely responsible for: (a) all federal, state, and local taxes on your income; (b) your own health, workers' compensation, and other insurance; (c) all business expenses including fuel, maintenance, docking fees, and equipment; and (d) compliance with all laws applicable to your services.

2. Eligibility Requirements

To register and remain active as a Service Provider, you must at all times:

  • Be at least 18 years of age and legally authorized to work in the United States;
  • Hold a valid U.S. Coast Guard Captain's License (if operating as a Captain) with all required endorsements for your vessel type and operating area;
  • Maintain current state registration for all listed vessels;
  • Maintain commercial marine liability insurance meeting or exceeding the minimum requirements of the state(s) in which you operate;
  • Provide accurate, current, and complete information about yourself and your vessels;
  • Have no disqualifying criminal history or pending charges that would make you unfit to provide services to the public.

3. Credential Verification

You authorize Splash Rides to verify your credentials, including your USCG license, government-issued ID, boat registration, and insurance certificates. Splash Rides' review is a limited administrative check of submitted documents only and does not constitute an endorsement, guarantee, or certification of your qualifications. You are solely responsible for maintaining the accuracy and currency of all submitted credentials. Any lapse, suspension, or revocation of required licenses or insurance must be reported to Splash Rides immediately, and you must cease providing services until the issue is resolved.

4. Commission Structure and Payments

Platform Fee: Splash Rides retains a 20% platform fee from each completed booking. You receive 80% of the total booking amount, less any applicable Stripe payment processing fees.

Payout Schedule: Earnings become available after payment capture and trip completion. Standard payouts are processed within 2–3 business days at no charge. Instant payouts are available for a 1.99% fee.

Stripe Connect: Payouts are processed via Stripe Connect. You must complete Stripe's onboarding process, including identity verification, before receiving payouts. You are responsible for providing accurate payout information. Splash Rides is not liable for delays caused by incorrect banking information or Stripe's processing timelines.

Taxes: You are solely responsible for reporting and paying all taxes on your earnings, including self-employment taxes. Splash Rides will issue a 1099-K if required by applicable law.

5. Vessel Requirements and Safety

  • All listed vessels must be seaworthy, properly maintained, and in full compliance with USCG and applicable state safety requirements;
  • All required safety equipment (life jackets, flares, fire extinguishers, etc.) must be present, accessible, and in serviceable condition;
  • You must conduct a comprehensive safety briefing with all passengers before departure;
  • Vessels must be accurately described in listings — any material misrepresentation may result in immediate account suspension;
  • You must not operate any vessel while impaired by alcohol, drugs, or any impairing substance.

6. Bookings and Cancellations

You agree to honor all confirmed bookings. If you must cancel a confirmed booking, you must do so through the Platform as early as possible. Repeated cancellations will negatively affect your visibility on the Platform and may result in account suspension. Splash Rides reserves the right to impose financial penalties for last-minute Provider cancellations that harm Renters.

7. All-Communications-On-Platform Policy

All communications with Renters must occur through the in-app messaging system. You may not solicit off-platform transactions, share personal contact information to circumvent Platform fees, or conduct repeat bookings outside the Platform. Violations may result in immediate permanent account termination and forfeiture of pending earnings.

8. Insurance Obligations

You must maintain, at minimum, commercial marine liability insurance with limits adequate for your state and operating area. You acknowledge that Splash Rides carries no insurance on your behalf, does not insure your vessel, and is not responsible for any damage to your vessel or injury to any person arising from your operations. You are strongly encouraged to consult with a marine insurance specialist to ensure adequate coverage for your specific operations.

9. Reviews and Ratings

Renters may rate and review their experience with you. You may not offer incentives in exchange for positive reviews or threaten Renters for negative reviews. Splash Rides reserves the right to remove reviews that violate its policies, but will not remove legitimate negative feedback.

10. Account Suspension and Termination

Splash Rides may suspend or terminate your Provider account immediately, with or without notice, for: (a) violation of this Agreement or the Terms of Service; (b) safety violations or incidents; (c) fraudulent or deceptive conduct; (d) consistent poor reviews or high cancellation rates; (e) lapse of required credentials or insurance; or (f) any conduct that Splash Rides reasonably believes harms users or the Platform's reputation. Upon termination, any pending earnings for completed, confirmed trips will be paid out per the standard schedule; earnings for unconfirmed or cancelled bookings may be withheld.

11. Indemnification

You agree to indemnify, defend, and hold harmless Splash Rides, Inc. and its officers, directors, employees, agents, and successors from all claims, liabilities, damages, losses, costs, and fees (including reasonable attorneys' fees) arising from: your provision of services; the condition, safety, or operation of your vessel; any injury, death, or property damage occurring during or related to your services; your violation of this Agreement or any applicable law; or any claim by a third party arising from your independent contractor activities.

12. Entire Agreement

This Provider Agreement, together with the Splash Rides Terms of Service and Privacy Policy, constitutes the entire agreement between you and Splash Rides regarding your role as a Service Provider. In the event of a conflict, this Provider Agreement controls with respect to Provider-specific obligations.
